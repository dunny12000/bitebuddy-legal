## AI-Powered Food Analysis
Our food analysis feature uses OpenAI's GPT-4 Vision API to identify foods and estimate nutritional content from photos. When you submit a food photo:
- The image is uploaded to our secure servers
- The image is sent to OpenAI for AI-powered analysis
- OpenAI processes the image and returns nutritional estimates
- Results are stored in your account for tracking purposes
**Important Notes:**
- Food photos are transmitted securely via HTTPS encryption
- OpenAI may use uploaded images in accordance with their data usage policy
- Nutritional estimates are AI-generated and may not be 100% accurate
- We recommend consulting healthcare professionals for medical nutrition advice
